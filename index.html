Frequently Asked Questions

Tech Urja 2k25 is a two-day technical and cultural fest organized by Khalsa College of Engineering & Technology, Amritsar. It includes over 50 exciting events such as technical challenges, cultural performances, and esports tournaments.

Tech Urja 2k25 will be held on February 20 and 21, 2025, at Khalsa College of Engineering & Technology, Amritsar.

Registration for all events at Tech Urja 2k25 is completely free, except for E-sports, which have registration charges.

Participants can register through our official website or contact the organizing team directly. Stay updated via our website and social media platforms for detailed instructions.

Anyone with a passion for technology, creativity, or gaming is welcome to participate. The fest is open to students and professionals from across the country.

The fest features technical events (coding, robotics, IoT challenges), cultural competitions (dance, music, drama), e-sports tournaments, workshops, startup showcases, and networking opportunities.

Yes, participants have the chance to win exciting cash prizes, trophies, and certificates in various competitions.

No, Tech Urja 2k25 does not provide accommodation. Participants are advised to make their own arrangements if traveling from outside Amritsar.

The detailed schedule will be available on the official website and shared on our social media pages. Follow us to stay up-to-date with all announcements.
